About us

Our story

Since 1998
Sinkroteksas has been active in the industrial machinery industry. Established as a small company specializing in the production of industrial machinery and process automation for bakeries and breweries, After gaining experience and technological knowledge, Sinkroteksas realized the potential of vacuum cooling and began to focus on its development. Over the past two decades, we have been devoted to researching and developing new technologies to meet the ever-changing needs of our customers. With a commitment to quality and customer satisfaction, we strive to keep our clients up-to-date with the latest advancements in industrial vacuum cooling technology.
